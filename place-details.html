Erbil Citadel

Erbil, Kurdistan Region

Image Gallery

Historical Description

The Erbil Citadel is a fortified settlement built on an ancient tell. It is considered one of the oldest continuously inhabited places in the world, with evidence of occupation dating back several millennia. The citadel has served as a cultural, political, and economic center throughout many civilizations including Assyrians, Medes, Persians, Ottomans, and modern Kurdish society.

Location Information

  • City: Erbil
  • Region: Kurdistan Region
  • Country: Iraq
  • Type: Citadel
  • UNESCO Status: World Heritage Site
